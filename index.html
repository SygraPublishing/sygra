Sygra Publishing is a Toronto based publisher that only concentrates on spiritual uplifting and inspirational genres.  We also accept work by authors who write books with an occult and paranormal thriller undertone as well.  We are an author friendly book publisher who also offers self-publishing services such as editing, proof reading, cover designs, book formating and marketing.

 

Our goal is to bring light to this dark world and to inspire the hearts of many, instilling hope and joy in those who seek to find the truth and who are interested in the magical workings of all that which is hidden and sacred.
